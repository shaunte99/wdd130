History

White Water Wonders was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate river explorers who wanted to share their love for white-water rafting with the world. What started as a small operation along the Colorado River has grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the country.

Over the years, we’ve guided thousands of adventurers through some of the most breathtaking rapids and scenic routes across the United States. Our commitment to safety, fun, and sustainability continues to drive our mission to make every trip a memory of a lifetime.
